IGCSM

Skill is the unified force of experience, intellect and passion in their operation.” John Ruskin. The Indira Gandhi Computer Saksharta Mission (IGCSM), founded in 2008 through the sheer effort of Dr. Saurabh Singhal, aims to Educate, Enable, and Empower. Operating across 21 states, IGCSM has impacted more than 3 Lakh lives by focusing on educating children, enhancing youth skills.

Experience

IGCSM Leadership & Expansion

Since 2008, spearheading IGCSM’s transformation into a leading organization in the Indian education sector, establishing strategic partnerships, and working with over 19 ministries.

Government & Strategic Collaborations

Nurtured partnerships with national and international organizations, including key roles in the Technical Intern Training Program (TITP). Worked closely with ministries.

Recognition & Awards

Achieved accolades from prestigious entities like NSDC, Microsoft, and various government and private organizations for significant contributions to education and skill development.

SGTU University Establishment

Founded Sikkim Global Technical University in 2023 to enhance educational opportunities, with a special focus on North-East India. SGTU oversees operations of over 100 training centers.

Educational Innovation

Developed industry-relevant curricula to prepare students for professional demands, while ensuring operational excellence across all training centers and educational programs.

Visionary Leadership

Expanded IGCSM to over 200 franchises, received numerous awards for contributions to education and social empowerment, and played a pivotal role in skill development initiatives at both national.
