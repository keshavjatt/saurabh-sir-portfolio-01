Guiding Force Behind IGCSM

Since 2008, under the visionary guidance of Dr. Saurabh Singhal, IGCSM has transformed from a humble initiative into a prominent force in the Indian education sector. The journey began with the establishment of the first franchise in Tijara, Rajasthan, and the inaugural institute in Jansath, Uttar Pradesh. Over the years, IGCSM has expanded its footprint to over 200 franchisees and numerous institutes, collaborating with more than 10 ministries to provide skill development and education to rural parts of India.

Dr. Singhal's relentless commitment to education and skill development has seen IGCSM form partnerships with both national and international organizations, playing a pivotal role in the Technical Intern Training Program (TITP). The organization has earned accolades from prestigious entities such as NSDC and Microsoft, as well as various government and private organizations.

Evolution of IGCSM and SGTU

In 2023, Dr. Singhal founded Sikkim Global Technical University (SGTU), marking a significant milestone in his journey. SGTU, along with multiple ITIs and polytechnics across India, now operates over 100 active training centers, making substantial contributions to government skill development initiatives at both national and state levels.

From modest beginnings, Dr. Singhal's leadership has propelled IGCSM to become a giant in the education sector, dedicated to empowering the needy and fostering development in rural India. Today, IGCSM stands as a testament to the power of vision, determination, and collaboration, impacting lives and creating opportunities across the nation.
